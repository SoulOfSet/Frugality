About Frugality

\tHave you ever bought something and wondered? Did I purchase that for a decent price? The television you bought, the lipstick, even something as petty as broccoli. Maybe you could have done better. Maybe you could have saved a buck or two. Maybe even hundreds! Did you know that retailers change their prices weekly, sometimes daily? This app is designed to keep you informed of these price changes. Frugality, which is co-op with one of the leading database companies in the country, is here to provide you with the best shopping tool ever.

BECOME A FRUGALMANIAC!!

\tFrugality understands the needs of the modern consumer in today’s digital age. Today’s shopper is bombarded with choices and, frankly, does not have the time to search for the best price available. That is what Frugality will do for you. The word Frugal means “sparing or economical with regard to money or food”. As the name implies, this app will help you to become frugal. Frugality will save you time and money by finding the best deal for you by searching top retailers and displaying the best price in your area of choice.
